Common Area

The common area of the dorm quickly after moving in became the most used room in our dorm. My roommates and I spend most of our time in the dorm in the common area watching TV, studying, or just talking.

Our common area commonly recieves other visitors, and I believe it has aided me in an evolved definition of home.

Activites

I love getting involved in clubs at my current school, and I believe that Honors Ambassadors and the Space Hardware Club have given me wonderful opportunites to improve myself.

My Current Residency turned Home

Welcome to the University of Alabama in Huntsville and how it aided to define home for me.

Projects

A defining feature that led me to choose UAH over other schools were the opportunities to learn and research. The University has yet to disappoint in this aspect.

I have had the opportunity to join the Space Hardware Club and participated in the Two-Month program. I got to build a rover, and I am currently trying to join other SHC teams.

Friends

Easily the most home-defining feature of UAH is the friends I have made. They have shown me other highly motivated people, and they are very passionate about their studies.

The many friends I get to see daily make UAH's workload seem not so miserable. They have helped me in many ways.
