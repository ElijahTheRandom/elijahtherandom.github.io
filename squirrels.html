Example One

With Trampoline

In this example we assume that the squirrels have become accustomed to using the trampoline to get their food from a neighboring tree.

After generations of squirrels utilizing the trampoline, the squirrels lose their ability to climb. Hopefully, the trampoline will be there forever!

Example Two

Without Trampoline

In this example we assume the trampoline from the previous example has been removed from between the trees.

Without the trampoline, the squirrels will fall to the ground. They will either die on impact or die due to other circumstances related to being stranded on the ground.
